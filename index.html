Unity UI dev’s guide to learning Unreal 5 (v0.1)

There are more similarity then difference in my experience of learning Unreal 5 when I have years of experience in Unity, and I hope this premier inspires you to learn something new.

Where to start

https://dev.epicgames.com/community/learning/courses/l3p/unreal-engine-your-first-hour-in-umg/6o5/introduction “Your First Hour in UMG” is the best beginner UMG(Unreal Motion Graphic) tutorial that I found. Don’t worry that it’s based in Unreal 4, there are interface differences, but learning how to work in UMG is the bases for more fancy stuff.
